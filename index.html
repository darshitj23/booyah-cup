üéÆ IN-GAME RULES

  • Character skills are strictly banned in game by creators of matches
  • Gun attributes are also banned into game by creators of matches
  • Load out and pet skills also banned
  • Player could not break the gloo wall of enemy in any condition
  • Player can not drop gloo wall for trapping enemy into the safe zone
  • Player can't climb on any thing in match
  • The player should only come straight
  • Any types of throwables are banned in matches
  • UAV drones and cheats are banned in matches

üìã OUT-GAME RULES

  • If player won a match, it gets 2 points and if lose, player gets 0 points
  • If any player won a match, his cutoff round (ex, 4-3, player win match by +1 CS and loser player gets -1 CS)
  • If both players are not available to playing match, both gets 0 CS and 1-1 points each
  • If any one player is not available for playing his match on his match date, it considered lose by 4-0 and gets 0 points and 0 CS
  • From matches section, player can see his matches particularly by select his name and press show matches button

‚≠ê SUPER-6 & FINALES SYSTEM

  • Top-3 players of each groups will be select to play into super-6
  • Super-6 has 15 matches and players also plays 2 matches per day
  • Group system will not be longer into super-6 system
  • Player can play matches with all selected players who are into super-6
  • Top 4 of super-6 will be able to play into finales
  • Finale system has 4 matches, qualifier-1 & 2, eliminator and final
